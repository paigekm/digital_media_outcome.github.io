Heaphy Track

Travel through expansive tussock downs, lush forests and nīkau palms to the roaring seas of the West Coast.

See if you can spot unique wildlife including spotted kiwi/roroa and takahē.

The human history of Heaphy Track dates back hundreds of years.

Try and spy the nocturnal carnivorous land snail around Heaphy Hut on a damp night.

Nelson Lakes

Explore the heart of the South Island!

Visit Angelus Hut which sits on the shores of Lake Angelus, surrounded by spectacular mountain views!

From May to October, the lake is frozen, snow blankets your route and you may have to dig your way to the hut!
